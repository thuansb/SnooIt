SnooIt Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 Aug 2024

At SnooIt, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Reminders Data:

SnooIt allows you to set reminders for web pages, which includes saving the URL, reminder time, and any notes you choose to add. This data is stored using chrome.storage.sync, enabling you to access your reminders across different devices signed into your Chrome account.

No Personal Identifiable Information (PII):

SnooIt does not collect or store any personal identifiable information beyond the reminder data you set. We do not access or store your browsing history or any other personal data.

2. Permissions and Data Handling

Permissions Used:

  • Storage: To save reminder data across sessions and devices.
  • Alarms: To schedule and manage reminder notifications.
  • Notifications: To alert you when your reminders are due.
  • ActiveTab: To access the URL of the current tab for setting reminders.

Data Security:

All reminder data is securely stored using Chrome's storage.sync API. This ensures that your data is synchronized across devices while maintaining security and privacy.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

SnooIt does not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties. All data processed by the extension is used solely for providing the core functionality of setting and managing reminders.

4. User Control

You have full control over your reminders and can edit or delete them at any time. Since we use chrome.storage.sync, any changes you make will be reflected across all your devices using the same Chrome account.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.
